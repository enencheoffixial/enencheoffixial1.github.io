About Me


Hello there and welcome to my Official Page My name is Nyito Terhemmen David AKA Dwi Se. I was born in kano State in Nigeria, we moved to Gboko Benue State Nigeria. But I based in Lagos currently.

I was born on the 2nd of of August, and am an upcoming singer and a song writer, this is where my passion is and I love what i do.

As for my Educational background, I attended Sora International School (Primary school), and i graduated in 2016, For my secondary School i attended Kusaki College and Graduated in 2022, all in Gboko Benue State Nigeria. Music is what I do best and I was self taught, I worked on myself byy following my fellow artist which are my bosses and I also try to freestyle on their songs while they sing.

With all my experience I realeased my first song Your_body.

And also Junny_ana which is a love song I sang out of anger.

And last for now Avoid_dem which I featured Oluwa Dadaz.
